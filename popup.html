Kindle Copy
This extension enhances your Kindle Cloud Reader experience by letting you easily copy text using OCR technology.

  • Copy entire page: Simply right-click anywhere on the page to copy all visible text
  • Select specific text: Highlight the desired text, right-click, and choose "Copy from Screen" to copy just that section
  • Also use (Command+C for mac and Ctrl+C for others) keyboard shortcut to copy
